Bio

I work on practical AI systems and research tooling, with interests in trustworthy AI, agentic AI, backdoor attacks, and deep learning security. I like building small systems with sharp feedback loops: clear metrics, reproducible experiments, simple interfaces, and enough automation to make the next iteration cheaper than the last one.

My recent work spans visual model evaluation workflows, productivity tools, and infrastructure utilities for monitoring compute availability. I am also building Drifty.
